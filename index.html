SKT Campaing

It's a story about Giants who want to rule Toril's world.
There are six races of giants: cloud giants, fire giants, frost giants, hill giants, stone giants, and storm giants.
Each of them desires to rule the world in their own way.

Danilo is our Dungeon Master! He guides us through the story based on SKT - Storm King's Thunder, an official adventure from Wizards of the Coast for the 5th edition. It creates an exciting and memorable play experience for the Storm King's Thunder story.
